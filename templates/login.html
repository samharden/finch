Small firms shouldn't be at a disadvantage.

When small firms litigate against big firms, the big firms have all the advantages of institutional knowledge. Our platform levels the playing field, letting small firms share their knowledge effectively and efficiently.

Common interest = Reason to share data.

Finch KM is designed for specialized bar and practice associations. Small firms with a common interest have every reason to share their insights and internal knowledge with the group.

Easy-to-use and intuitive.

Search across counties, case types, even inside the motions and depositions for the information you need. The less time you spend combing through documents, the more time you spend working on the things that matter.

Accessible from anywhere.

Lawyers love email. We're not looking to change that. You can use the full functionality of Finch from email, post discussions, and get notifications. Finch will even recommend discussions and documents to you via email. Our secure web-based platform lets you access the materials you need from any device, anywhere. There's nothing to install. Finch KM works seamlessly across all devices.

Powerfully connected data

Intuitive Search

Searching through an email listserve is a pain. Our search feature lets you quickly find relevant discussions and documents in the right jurisdiction and practice area.

Connected Data

Finch KM automatically makes connections between discussions and documents, letting you find things quicker and easier.

Relevant Content

Easy Discussions

Private and Secure
